Bounty Brawl

Detail

           Bounty Brawl is a fast-paced multiplayer action roguelite shooter developed with Unity3D and Photon Fusion.

Responsibility

           Implemented gameplay and system features, including an enemy perk system, key binding system, cloud save feature, and gameplay achievement system integrated with Steam Achievements. Improved boss and minion attack states within the existing FSM-based enemy AI, polished gameplay features, and fixed Photon Fusion networking/connection issues.
