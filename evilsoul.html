Evil Soul

Detail

           Evil Soul is an Online Idle RPG Game.

Responsibility

           In my role, I've managed and developed the game "Evil Soul" throughout its entire lifecycle. I implemented core gameplay systems, including the combat system, skill system, Character behavior, shop mechanics, and player data structures. Additionally, I developed Skill creator tools for designers using XNode. I utilized Unity Addressable for efficient asset management in projects. Furthermore, I managed and published both Closed Beta and Open Beta versions on the Play Store and App Store.
