League of Bomb

Detail

           League of Bomb is a Bomberman-inspired multiplayer action game where players ride dragons, place bombs, and use unique skills to defeat opponents in chaotic real-time battles.

Responsibility

           Developed player AI / bot behavior for multiplayer matches using Photon Quantum. Combined State Machine and Behavior Tree approaches using Photon Quantum BT tools. Designed AI behaviors such as gathering power-ups, escaping from players, and hunting players.
